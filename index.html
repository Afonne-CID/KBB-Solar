Facts

The panels are usually facing the wrong direction; for efficient charging of the batteries, the panels should be faced in the right direction.
The wrong size of wires is used to connect solar panels; if you need to take energy from the Sun then we need very thick special solar panel wires.
The solar panels are normally not connected in a power voltage charging style; this is required for the fastest and most efficient charging of batteries.
The distance between the solar panels and the inverter is not always considered by installers; the longer this distance the higher the resistance in the setup system which reduces the battery charging efficiency.
Many installers never put into consideration the users power need consumption; in order to install an efficient solar system, you always need to design the system specific to the users power consumption needs.
